Kimchi Jjigae

Time taken: Approximately 40 minutes

Number of calories: Approximately 250 calories per serving

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups kimchi, chopped
  • 1/2 lb pork belly or pork shoulder, thinly sliced
  • 4 cups water or anchovy stock
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 block tofu, cut into cubes
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Vegetable oil, for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add sliced pork belly (or pork shoulder) to the pot. Cook until browned.
  3. Add minced garlic and chopped onion to the pot. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add chopped kimchi, gochujang, and soy sauce to the pot. Stir well to combine.
  5. Pour water or anchovy stock into the pot. Bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 20-25 minutes.
  7. Add tofu cubes to the pot. Simmer for an additional 5 minutes.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Garnish with chopped green onions.
  10. Remove from heat and serve hot.
