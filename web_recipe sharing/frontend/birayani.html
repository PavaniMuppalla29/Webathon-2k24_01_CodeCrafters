Chicken Biryani

Time Taken:

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Cooking Time: 1 hour

Calories Intake:

Calories per serving: Approximately 300-400 calories per serving (varies based on portion size)

Chicken Biryani Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Chicken (with bone) - 500g
  • Basmati rice - 2 cups
  • Onion (sliced) - 2 medium
  • Tomato (chopped) - 1 large
  • Yogurt - 1/2 cup
  • Ginger garlic paste - 2 tbsp
  • Green chilies (slit) - 2
  • Mint leaves - 1/2 cup
  • Cilantro leaves - 1/2 cup
  • Biryani masala powder - 2 tbsp
  • Turmeric powder - 1/4 tsp
  • Red chili powder - 1 tsp
  • Garam masala powder - 1 tsp
  • Bay leaf - 2
  • Cloves - 4
  • Cinnamon stick - 1 inch
  • Cardamom pods - 3
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil - 3 tbsp
  • Ghee (clarified butter) - 2 tbsp

Instructions:

  • Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger garlic paste, biryani masala powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, salt, and oil for 30 minutes.
  • Soak basmati rice in water for 30 minutes, then drain.
  • Heat ghee in a pan, add bay leaf, cloves, cinnamon stick, and cardamom pods.
  • Add sliced onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  • Add tomatoes, green chilies, mint leaves, cilantro leaves, and salt. Cook until tomatoes turn soft.
  • Add marinated chicken and cook until it is half done.
  • In a separate pot, boil water with salt and cook soaked rice until it is 70% cooked.
  • Layer the cooked chicken gravy and partially cooked rice in a large pot, alternating layers.
  • Drizzle saffron milk on top, cover with a lid, and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes until the rice is fully cooked and the flavors are absorbed.
  • Chicken Biryani is ready to be served. Enjoy it hot with raita or salad.
