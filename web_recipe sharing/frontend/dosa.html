Dosa Recipe

Time Taken:

Preparation Time: 8-10 hours (for fermenting batter)

Cooking Time: 2-3 minutes per dosa

Calories Intake:

Calories per serving: Approximately 100-150 calories per dosa (varies based on size and ingredients)

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Urad dal (black gram lentils)
  • Fenugreek seeds
  • Salt
  • Oil or ghee (clarified butter)

Instructions:

  1. Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately for 6-8 hours.
  2. Grind them together into a smooth batter consistency.
  3. Add salt to the batter and let it ferment for 8-10 hours or overnight.
  4. Heat a dosa tawa (griddle) and pour a ladleful of batter on it.
  5. Spread the batter in a circular motion to form a thin pancake.
  6. Drizzle oil or ghee around the edges and cook until golden brown.
  7. Flip the dosa and cook for a minute on the other side.
  8. Serve hot with chutney, sambar, or any desired accompaniment.
