Focaccia Recipe

Time Taken:

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time: 20-25 minutes

Calories Intake:

Calories per serving: Approximately 200-250 calories (varies based on size and toppings)

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Herbs and spices (rosemary, thyme, garlic, etc.)
  • Sea salt flakes (for topping)

Instructions:

  • In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, yeast, salt, and water. Mix until a dough forms.
  • Knead the dough on a floured surface until smooth and elastic.
  • Transfer the dough to a greased baking pan and press it out to cover the bottom of the pan.
  • Drizzle olive oil over the dough and sprinkle with herbs, spices, and sea salt flakes.
  • Cover the dough and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  • Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  • Bake the focaccia in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  • Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly before slicing and serving.
