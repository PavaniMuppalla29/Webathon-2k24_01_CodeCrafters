Arancini Recipe

Time Taken:

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 25-30 minutes

Calories Intake:

Calories per serving: Approximately 150-200 calories per arancini (varies based on size and filling)

Ingredients:

  • Arborio rice
  • Chicken or vegetable broth
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  • Cook the arborio rice in chicken or vegetable broth according to package instructions until tender.
  • Stir in grated Parmesan cheese and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Let the rice cool slightly, then shape it into balls or cones, placing a cube of mozzarella cheese in the center of each.
  • Roll each arancini in flour, then dip in beaten eggs, and finally coat with breadcrumbs.
  • Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350°F (180°C).
  • Fry the arancini in batches until golden brown and crispy, about 3-4 minutes per batch.
  • Remove from the oil and drain on paper towels.
  • Serve hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and marinara sauce for dipping.
