Ossobucco Recipe

Time Taken:

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 2-3 hours

Calories Intake:

Calories per serving: Approximately 400-500 calories (varies based on portion size)

Ingredients:

  • Veal shanks
  • All-purpose flour
  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Celery
  • Garlic
  • Tomato paste
  • Beef or chicken broth
  • White wine
  • Bay leaf
  • Thyme
  • Salt and pepper
  • Gremolata (optional for garnish)

Instructions:

  • Season the veal shanks with salt and pepper, then dredge them in flour.
  • Heat olive oil in a large skillet or Dutch oven over medium-high heat.
  • Brown the veal shanks on all sides, then remove and set aside.
  • Add chopped onion, carrot, celery, and garlic to the skillet. Cook until softened.
  • Stir in tomato paste and cook for another minute.
  • Return the veal shanks to the skillet and pour in broth and white wine.
  • Add bay leaf and thyme, then bring to a simmer.
  • Cover and cook over low heat for 2-3 hours, or until the meat is tender and falling off the bone.
  • Remove the bay leaf and thyme sprigs before serving.
  • Garnish with gremolata if desired, and serve hot.
