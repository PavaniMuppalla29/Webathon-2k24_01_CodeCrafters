Teokbokki

Time taken: Approximately 30 minutes

Number of calories: Approximately 350 calories per serving

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Korean rice cakes (tteok)
  • 1/2 cup gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • 3 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 cup fish cakes, sliced (optional)
  • 1/2 cup cabbage, shredded (optional)
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1 boiled egg (optional)
  • Sesame seeds, for garnish
  • Vegetable oil, for cooking

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet or pot, heat vegetable oil over medium heat.
  2. Add gochujang, soy sauce, and sugar to the skillet. Stir well to combine.
  3. Add water or vegetable broth to the skillet and bring to a simmer.
  4. Add Korean rice cakes (tteok) to the skillet. Cook for 8-10 minutes, or until the rice cakes are tender.
  5. If using, add fish cakes and shredded cabbage to the skillet. Cook for an additional 3-4 minutes.
  6. Garnish with chopped green onions, boiled egg (if desired), and sesame seeds.
  7. Remove from heat and serve hot.
