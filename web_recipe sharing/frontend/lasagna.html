Lasagna Recipe

Time Taken:

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Cooking Time: 1 hour

Calories Intake:

Calories per serving: Approximately 400-500 calories (varies based on portion size)

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Italian sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Egg
  • Italian seasoning
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil (optional for garnish)

Instructions:

  • Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  • In a large skillet, cook ground beef, Italian sausage, onion, and garlic until browned.
  • Stir in tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  • In a separate bowl, mix together ricotta cheese, egg, and Italian seasoning.
  • Spread a layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
  • Arrange a layer of cooked lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  • Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, then sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  • Repeat layers, ending with a final layer of noodles and sauce on top.
  • Cover with foil and bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for 45 minutes.
  • Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  • Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
  • Garnish with fresh basil if desired, and enjoy!
