Pasta Recipe

Time Taken:

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Cooking Time: 10-12 minutes

Calories Intake:

Calories per serving: Approximately 250-350 calories (varies based on sauce and portion size)

Ingredients:

  • Pasta (spaghetti, penne, fettuccine, etc.)
  • Pasta sauce (marinara, Alfredo, pesto, etc.)
  • Vegetables (tomatoes, bell peppers, mushrooms, etc.)
  • Protein (chicken, shrimp, tofu, etc.) - optional
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Herbs and spices (basil, oregano, red pepper flakes, etc.)
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  • Add the pasta and cook according to package instructions until al dente.
  • While the pasta is cooking, heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  • Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes.
  • Add any additional vegetables or protein to the skillet and cook until tender and cooked through.
  • Once the pasta is cooked, drain it and add it to the skillet with the sauce.
  • Toss everything together until the pasta is evenly coated with the sauce.
  • Season with salt, pepper, and herbs/spices to taste.
  • Serve hot, garnished with grated cheese and fresh herbs if desired.
