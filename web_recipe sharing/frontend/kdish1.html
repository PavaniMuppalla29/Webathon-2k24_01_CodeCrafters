Time Taken:

Approximately 20 minutes

Number of Calories:

Approximately 300 calories per serving

Kimchi Fried Rice Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup kimchi, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons kimchi juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 2 eggs
  • Soy sauce, to taste
  • Sesame seeds, for garnish
  • Vegetable oil, for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and chopped kimchi to the skillet. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add cooked rice and kimchi juice to the skillet. Stir well to combine.
  4. Push the rice mixture to one side of the skillet and crack the eggs into the other side.
  5. Scramble the eggs until cooked, then mix them into the rice.
  6. Add chopped green onions and sesame oil to the skillet. Stir to combine.
  7. Season with soy sauce to taste. Garnish with sesame seeds.
  8. Remove from heat and serve hot.
