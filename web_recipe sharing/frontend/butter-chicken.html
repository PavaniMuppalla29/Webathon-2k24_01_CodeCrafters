Time Taken:

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 40 minutes minutes

Calories Intake:

Calories per serving: Approximately 150-200 calories per arancini (varies based on size and filling)

Butter Chicken Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Chicken (boneless) - 500g
  • Yogurt - 1 cup
  • Ginger garlic paste - 2 tbsp
  • Butter - 3 tbsp
  • Tomato puree - 1 cup
  • Cream - 1/4 cup
  • Kashmiri red chili powder - 1 tsp
  • Garam masala powder - 1 tsp
  • Kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves) - 1 tbsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Cilantro leaves (for garnishing) - optional

Instructions:

  • Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger garlic paste, and salt for 1 hour.
  • Heat butter in a pan, add marinated chicken, and cook until it turns golden brown.
  • Add tomato puree, Kashmiri red chili powder, and garam masala powder. Cook until the gravy thickens.
  • Finish with cream and kasuri methi. Garnish with cilantro leaves if desired.
  • Serve hot with naan or rice.
