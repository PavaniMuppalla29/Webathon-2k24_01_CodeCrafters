Egg Drop Soup Recipe

Time Taken: 15 minutes

Calories: 100 per serving

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  • In a small bowl, dissolve cornstarch in water and set aside.
  • In a pot, bring chicken broth to a boil. Stir in grated ginger, soy sauce, and sliced green onions. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Once the broth is boiling, slowly pour in the beaten eggs while stirring the soup in a clockwise direction. The eggs will form thin ribbons in the broth.
  • Reduce heat to low and stir in the cornstarch mixture. Simmer for a few more minutes until the soup thickens slightly.
  • Remove from heat and serve hot. Garnish with additional sliced green onions if desired.
