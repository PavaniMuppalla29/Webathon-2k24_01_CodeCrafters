Bibimbap

Time taken: Approximately 40 minutes

Number of calories: Approximately 500 calories per serving

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 4 cups mixed vegetables (spinach, carrots, bean sprouts, zucchini, mushrooms)
  • 4 eggs
  • 4 tablespoons gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • 4 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 4 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 4 teaspoons rice vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Sesame seeds, for garnish
  • Vegetable oil, for cooking
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the rice according to package instructions and keep warm.
  2. Prepare the vegetables by blanching or saut√©ing them separately. Season with salt and set aside.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat. Fry the eggs sunny-side up or as desired.
  4. In a small bowl, mix together gochujang, sesame oil, soy sauce, rice vinegar, and minced garlic to make the bibimbap sauce.
  5. To serve, divide the warm rice among bowls. Arrange the cooked vegetables and fried eggs on top of the rice.
  6. Drizzle the bibimbap sauce over the top and garnish with sesame seeds.
  7. Mix everything together before eating.
