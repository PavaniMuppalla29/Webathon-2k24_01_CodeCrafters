Honey Garlic Chicken Recipe

Time Taken: 40 minutes

Calories: 350 per serving

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: Sesame seeds and chopped green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a baking dish with olive oil.
  • In a small bowl, mix together honey, soy sauce, ketchup, and minced garlic.
  • Place the chicken breasts in the baking dish. Season with salt and pepper.
  • Pour the honey garlic sauce over the chicken breasts, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  • Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through, basting with the sauce halfway through.
  • Once cooked, remove from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes.
  • Serve the honey garlic chicken hot, garnished with sesame seeds and chopped green onions if desired.
