Pizza Recipe

Time Taken:

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time: 12-15 minutes

Calories Intake:

Calories per serving: Approximately 300-400 calories (varies based on toppings and portion size)

Ingredients:

  • Dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Cheese (mozzarella, parmesan, etc.)
  • Toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, peppers, onions, etc.)
  • Olive oil
  • Herbs and spices (oregano, basil, garlic powder, etc.)

Instructions:

  • Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  • Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  • Transfer the dough to a pizza pan or baking sheet.
  • Spoon pizza sauce onto the dough and spread it evenly, leaving a border around the edges.
  • Sprinkle cheese over the sauce, followed by your desired toppings.
  • Drizzle a little olive oil over the pizza.
  • Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  • Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
